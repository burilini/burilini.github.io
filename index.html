Bio

I have been working in business for highly regulated companies for 20+ years. I received my MBA in 2002. As part of my Master's, I did some additional training in data structures and configuration code writing. I immediately applied that to improving the movement of data within my organization. Even though it scared several co-workers. (They came around eventually.)

So, here we are today. After participating and then leading several ERP integrations in my career, I am committed to learning the coding of that structure beginning to end. My interest and passion will always be how data and infrastructure can effectively move business forward. I can be contacted at the social media links below. And check me out at my GitHub handle : burilini

Willow River Trolley

This animation shows you stop by stop the Willow River Trolley route in scenic Willow River State Park, St. Croix County WI. The marker updates to the next stop automatically and progresses to the end. It is triggered off of GPS coordinates that are available through the St. Croix County Park Transportation site.
